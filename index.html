Microwave-Assisted Green Solvolysis of Wind Turbine Blades

About the Project

This study presents a groundbreaking approach to addressing the challenge of decommissioned wind turbine blades. Traditional recycling methods are often energy-intensive or fail to preserve fiber integrity.

Our pathway proposes to convert composite blade waste into valuable, high-performance materials using a microwave-assisted solvolysis process with a green solvent mixture.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To revolutionize wind turbine blade recycling through sustainable, energy-efficient solutions that reduce environmental impact and promote a circular economy.

Our Vision

To lead the future of green recycling technologies worldwide, turning industrial waste into valuable resources that empower sustainable industries.

Final Products

Photoluminescent Safety Tiles

High-performance tiles glowing in the dark for safety applications in low-light conditions.

Rubber Industrial Floor Blocks

Durable, sustainable floor blocks for industrial and commercial settings.
